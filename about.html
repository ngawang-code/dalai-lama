Biography

Tenzin Gyatso, the 14th Dalai Lama, was born in 1935 in Tibet and recognized as the reincarnation of the previous Dalai Lama. A symbol of peace and spiritual resilience, he has spent his life advocating for nonviolence, compassion, and human values.
